About AdCrush

This gallery showcases AI-generated advertisements created using the AdCrush platform.

The Process:

  1. Brand Analysis: We analyze your brand identity, values, and target audience
  2. Persona Development: AI generates detailed customer personas with unique pain points and hooks
  3. Style Variants: Multiple visual styles are created aligned with your brand aesthetic
  4. Ad Generation: High-quality images are generated with semantic tags for easy discovery
  5. Smart Gallery: Browse, search, and filter ads with powerful fuzzy search

Features:

  • search Fuzzy Search: Find ads even with typos
  • bolt Crushes: Save your favorites with one click
  • local_offer Semantic Tags: AI-generated marketing tags for each ad
  • bar_chart Analytics: Visualize tag distributions and trends
  • download Batch Download: Download multiple ads at once

bolt Curation Workflow

The fastest way to get exactly what you want:

  1. Browse & Crush: Click the bolt icon on ads you like to save them as "Crushes"
  2. Share Your Feedback: Go to Menu → Share Crushes and tell us:
    • What you love about these ads
    • What changes you'd like (colors, text, style, personas, etc.)
    • Any specific requests or variations you want
  3. Get Your Custom Batch: We'll create a new deck based on your crushes and feedback - perfectly tailored to what works for you!

tips_and_updates Pro Tip: The more crushes you share with specific feedback, the better we can refine your next batch!
